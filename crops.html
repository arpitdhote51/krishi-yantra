Crops Planted

Wheat

Wheat is one of the most widely grown crops in the world, used primarily for food. It thrives in temperate climates.

Rice

Rice is a staple food for over half of the world's population. It requires warm weather and plenty of water.

Corn

Corn is a versatile crop used for food, animal feed, and biofuel. It grows best in sunny conditions.
